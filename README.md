## 🧠 About Us

**Cyber Squad Academy** is an educational platform dedicated to teaching **technology**, **programming**, and **cybersecurity awareness** in a simple and practical way.

> 🎯 Our goal is to help learners build strong fundamentals, work on real projects, and grow their technical skills — **step by step**.

| 🔹 | Details |
|----|---------|
| 👨‍💻 **Founded By** | Aditya Kumar Mishra |
| 🎯 **Mission** | Education & Skill Development |
| 👥 **Audience** | Students, Beginners & Aspiring Developers |
| 🌍 **Reach** | Open to everyone, everywhere |
